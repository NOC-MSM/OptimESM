# OptimESM WP5 - Ocean Sea Ice Diagnostics

## Description:
Ocean sea-ice diagnostics calculated using NEMO Pipeline, an open-source Python library dedicated to creating reproducible diagnostic pipelines using outputs from NEMO ocean general circulation models (https://github.com/NOC-MSM/nemo_pipeline).

## Diagnostics:

Calculated diagnostics to date:
* AMOC at 26.5°N [Sv]
* OHT at 26.5°N [PW]
* OFT at 26.5°N [Sv]
* AMOC at OSNAP [Sv]
* OHT at OSNAP [PW]
* OFT at OSNAP [PW]
* Total NH Sea-Ice Area [m2]
* Total SH Sea-Ice Area [m2]
* Total SPNA Sea-Ice Area [m2]
* Total GIN Sea-Ice Area [m2]
* Total AO Sea-Ice Area [m2]
* Total SPNA Sea-Ice Volume [m3]
* Total GIN Sea-Ice Volume [m3]
* Total AO Sea-Ice Volume [m3]
* SPNA Mixed Layer Volume [m3]
* GIN Mixed Layer Volume [m3]
* AO Mixed Layer Volume [m3]
* SPNA Mixed Layer Depth [m]
* GIN Mixed Layer Depth [m]
* AO Mixed Layer Depth [m]

For full details of available diagnostics, including location of resulting output files, see:
